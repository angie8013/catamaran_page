La Perla: A Traditional Sailing Experience

Monohull

"La Perla Del Caribe" is a 43-foot Dufour Gib Sea Monohull. It features 4 double cabins. It accommodates up to 6 passengers. There are three private cabins with double beds and one private cabin with two single bunks. In the salon there's an additional double bed. The boat has a spacious kitchen and all necessary safety equipment so you can relax on your trip. There's music equipment and snorkel gear for passengers.

  • Luxury traditional sailboat
  • 3 double cabins + 1 with bunks
  • Equipped kitchen
  • 2 shared bathrooms
  • Dining room, relaxation space
  • Capacity: 12 (day trip) / 8 (overnight)
