Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to San Blas from Panama City?

You can reach San Blas by 4x4 vehicle (approximately 2.5 hours) or by small plane (30 minutes). We offer ground transportation service for an additional cost of $50 USD per person.

What should I bring for my San Blas experience?

We recommend bringing: biodegradable sunscreen, insect repellent, hat, sunglasses, swimsuit, towel, light clothing, water shoes, camera (preferably waterproof), and cash for additional expenses.

Is there internet coverage in San Blas?

Coverage is limited in the archipelago. Some islands have mobile signal, but it's intermittent. We recommend disconnecting and enjoying the experience.

Is it safe to travel to San Blas?

Yes, San Blas is a safe destination. The archipelago is administered by the Guna people, who maintain strict control over tourism. Our vessels comply with all maritime safety protocols.
