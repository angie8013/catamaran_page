About Us

Our Story

After more than 16 years sailing seas around the world, we found in San Blas a magical corner that captivated us. Here we decided to drop anchor and share this paradise with those seeking something more than just a destination: a real connection with nature, Guna culture, and the freedom to live at the rhythm of the sea.

Catamaran 360 was born among friends, with the dream of offering something authentic, away from traditional tourism. We believe in experiences that respect the environment, support local communities, and flow with good energy. Each journey is designed to make you feel part of something special, sailing with consciousness and an open heart.

Today, we feel grateful to be part of San Blas and its magic. We continue sharing the sea with an adventurous spirit, sustainable approach, and a family touch that makes each trip unique. Because more than tours, what we offer are moments that stay with you.

Mission

To offer unforgettable nautical experiences that connect our visitors with the natural beauty of San Blas and the cultural richness of the Guna people, promoting responsible and sustainable tourism that benefits local communities.

Vision

To be recognized as the leading company in sustainable nautical experiences in Panama, establishing a standard of excellence in service, cultural respect, and environmental conservation that inspires the tourism industry.

Values

  • Respect for nature and local cultures
  • Authenticity in every experience
  • Safety and professionalism
  • Hospitality and personalized attention
  • Commitment to sustainability

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Catamaran 360 we firmly believe that tourism should be a positive force for local communities and the environment. That's why we commit to:

  • Minimize our environmental impact: We use solar energy, reduce plastic use, and responsibly manage our waste.
  • Support Guna communities: We work directly with local communities, respecting their traditions and contributing to their economy.
  • Educate our visitors: We share knowledge about the marine ecosystem and Guna culture to foster respect and conservation.
  • Responsible practices: We follow strict protocols for marine life observation and anchoring in safe zones.
