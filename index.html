Explore San Blas Islands: Unforgettable Catamaran Tours and Overnight Stays

We are a group of sailors with free souls who have been traveling the seas for more than 16 years. Today, San Blas is our home: a natural paradise where we live in harmony with the Guna Yala community and the peaceful rhythm of the sea. We believe in travels that connect, that respect the land and honor local culture.

At Catamaran 360 we sail with consciousness. We create sustainable, authentic experiences full of good vibes. We share sunsets, music, snorkeling, seafood, and moments that feel like family. More than a tour, it's a way to live the sea with an open heart.
